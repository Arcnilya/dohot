This study does not raise ethical concerns, as it was conducted with no abuse of public infrastructure and no human participants were involved. 
Additionally, while planning the study, we minimize any potential burden  on services such as resolvers or the Tor-network.The foremost issue here being repeatedly building and tearing down Tor-circuits and the impact it could have on the Tor-network itself. Fortunately the scope of this study remained limited enough to fall under normal usage. The same remains true for any resolvers used and traffic sent.
