\section{Ethical Considerations}

This study raises no ethical conserns as all considered issues have been deemed insignificant in this context.

The study operated purely on public data and utilized no human participants.
Additionally while planning the study we considered the risks related to increased loads 
on services such as resolvers or the Tor-network.The foremost issue here being 
repeatedly building and tearing down Tor-circuits and the impact it could have on
the Tor-network itself.Fortunately the scope of this study remained limited enough to
fall under normal usage. The same remains true for any resolvers used and traffic sent.
